Pricing

Get rewarded for recycling with our competitive rates across India.

Pricing Tiers

We offer competitive rates based on the type of waste and its condition. Here are our general pricing ranges in India:

Electronics

Up to ₹3,000 per item depending on the condition.

Plastics

₹5 to ₹150 per kg based on type and recyclability.

Metals

₹200 to ₹1,500 per kg depending on the metal type (e.g., copper, aluminum).

Paper

Up to ₹20 per kg for recyclable paper products.

Glass

Between ₹5 to ₹100 per kg depending on glass quality and type.

How We Calculate Prices

Prices are determined based on several factors, including:

  • Item Condition
  • Recyclability of the Material
  • Current Market Rates for Recyclables in India
  • Processing and Transportation Costs
